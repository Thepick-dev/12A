LET'S BE HAVING YOU

Game Rules

  • Pay ten of your finest English pounds to enter.
  • At the start of each Gameweek, pick a team you think will win. Each team can only be picked once except for 2 pre-selected teams you can pick twice. Win points are based on Table below. Teams are split into quartiles based on league position at the start of the week. Be brave – cowardice will get you nowhere!
  • Pick a player each week. They earn points for goals and assists. Use Haaland wisely!
  • The winner is the player with the most points at the end of the season.
  • What constitutes a Gameweek is at the organiser's discretion and won’t necessarily follow other popular fantasy games.
