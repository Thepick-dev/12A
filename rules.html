LMS XII

LET'S BE HAVING YOU

PAY TEN OF YOUR FINEST ENGLISH POUNDS TO ENTER.

AT THE START OF EACH GAME WEEK, PICK A TEAM YOU THINK WILL WIN THAT WEEK. YOU CAN ONLY PICK EACH TEAM ONCE THROUGHOUT THE COMPETITION, EXCEPT FOR TWO PRE-SELECTED TEAMS THAT YOU CAN PICK TWICE.

EARN POINTS BASED ON YOUR TEAM'S RESULT AND THE DIFFICULTY OF THE MATCH. TEAMS ARE DIVIDED INTO QUARTILES BASED ON LEAGUE POSITION AT THE START OF THE GAME WEEK. AWAY WINS AGAINST HIGHER-QUARTILE TEAMS ARE MORE VALUABLE THAN HOME WINS AGAINST LOWER-QUARTILE TEAMS — SO BE DARING!

ALSO PICK ONE PLAYER EACH WEEK. YOUR PLAYER EARNS POINTS FOR ANY GOALS OR ASSISTS THEY GET. USE HAALAND WISELY!

WHAT COUNTS AS A GAMEWEEK IS ENTIRELY AT THE ORGANISER’S DISCRETION. IT MAY NOT FOLLOW THE TIMING OF OTHER FANTASY LEAGUE GAMES.

THE COMPETITION LASTS UNTIL THE END OF THE SEASON, AND THE PLAYER WITH THE MOST POINTS WINS THE GRAND PRIZE.

BE BRAVE – COWARDICE WILL GET YOU NOWHERE!
