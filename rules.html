LET'S BE HAVING YOU

Game Rules

  • Each week, pick one player and one team.
  • Player Points: Goal = 30 points, Assist = 15 points.
  • Team Points: Base points depend on result and venue:
    • Win (Home, same-quartile) = 60 points
    • Win (Away, same-quartile) = 70 points
    • Draw = 30% of a home win (H=18, A=20)
    • Loss = 0
  • Quartile Difficulty Bonus: Opponent Quartile – Your Team Quartile:
    • +3 = +30 pts
    • +2 = +20 pts
    • +1 = +10 pts
    • 0 = 0 pts
    • −1 = −10 pts
    • −2 = −20 pts
    • −3 = −30 pts
    • Negative bonuses removed on draws
